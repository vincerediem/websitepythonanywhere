RSI-Based Trading Strategy

Customize your RSI trading strategy by entering the parameters below:

  • Stock Symbol: Enter the symbol of the stock you want to trade.
  • RSI Period (Default: 14): Set the period for calculating the Relative Strength Index (RSI).
  • Buy RSI Threshold: Specify the RSI threshold for triggering buy signals (e.g., 30).
  • Sell RSI Threshold: Specify the RSI threshold for triggering sell signals (e.g., 70).
  • Start Date: Enter the start date for backtesting your strategy in YYYY-MM-DD format.
  • End Date: Enter the end date for backtesting your strategy in YYYY-MM-DD format.
  • Initial Account Balance (Default: $100,000): Set your initial account balance for trading.

Once you've customized the parameters and submitted the form, the algorithm will run with your chosen settings. Remember that trading involves risks, and your strategy's performance may vary based on your inputs.
